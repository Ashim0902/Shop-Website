Located in the bustling area of Pokhara-32, Sathmuhna, our hardware shop, established by RamRaj Adhikari in 2073 B.S., is your one-stop destination for all your construction and renovation needs. We offer a wide selection of high-quality tools, materials, and supplies tailored to both professional contractors and DIY enthusiasts. With a commitment to exceptional customer service, our knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist you in finding the right products for your projects. Whether you’re looking for power tools, plumbing supplies, or building materials, we take pride in providing everything you need to complete your tasks efficiently and effectively.

At our hardware shop in Pokhara-32, Sathmuhna, we pride ourselves on offering a comprehensive range of products to meet all your construction and renovation needs. In addition to high-quality tools and building materials, we provide various types of sand essential for construction, such as fine sand, coarse sand, and pit sand. Each type serves a specific purpose, from plastering to concrete production. We also offer a selection of cements, ensuring that you have the right materials to achieve durable and reliable results in your projects. Whether you’re a professional contractor or a DIY enthusiast, we are here to supply everything you need to bring your vision to life.
